Who We Are

Voxxed is a knowledge sharing platform, and overseer of Voxxed Days events. Each shares the same DNA as the Devoxx conferences, with a core focus on delivering top quality content for audiences local and global.

Content on Voxxed is generated by the developer community. Our categories reflect the most popular tracks types found at Voxxed Days and Devoxx conferences. You’ll be able to find videos from events, interviews and written articles that arm the busy the developer with knowledge that helps them improve and expand their experiences.

If you would like to get involved, please click the submit video button on the homepage and provide us some background info on it.
